Frequently Asked Questions

What makes RDXs vehicle storage unique?
RDX provides secure, climate-controlled, and 24/7 monitored vault storage for rare and exotic vehicles. We treat every car like a masterpiece, with tailored storage conditions and security measures to preserve their value.
Do you offer any detailing services?
Yes! We offer premium detailing services, including paint correction, interior restoration, and concours-level finishing to make your vehicle shine like new. Whether you need a quick clean-up or a full restoration, weve got you covered.
Can I schedule a car wrap with RDX?
Absolutely. Our expert team offers bespoke car wraps, including full color changes, matte finishes, and chrome deletes. We ensure your vehicle is not only protected but also styled to perfection.
How does your Paint Protection Film (PPF) work?
Our PPF is a high-quality, self-healing film that protects your car's paint from scratches, chips, and contaminants. Its practically invisible, providing an extra layer of defense while maintaining the vehicles pristine appearance.
Can I visit the RDX vault and see my car?
Yes, you can visit your vehicle at any time. We offer private viewing appointments to ensure a personal experience, with security and privacy always at the forefront.
How do I schedule a vehicle pickup or delivery?
We offer secure, enclosed transport services to pick up and deliver your vehicle wherever you need it. Just let us know the details, and well handle the rest, ensuring your car arrives safely and on time.
Do you offer any photography services for my vehicle?
Yes, we provide professional-grade photography for your vehicle. Whether you want to document its beauty or showcase it for an event, well make sure its captured in its best light.
